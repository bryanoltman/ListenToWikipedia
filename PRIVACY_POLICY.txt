Privacy Policy for Listen to Wikipedia

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Listen to Wikipedia does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

What the app does:
- Connects to a public WebSocket service (wikimon.hatnote.com) to receive a real-time stream of Wikipedia edits. This connection does not transmit any information about you.
- Stores your preferences (selected languages, audio settings) locally on your device using standard system storage. These preferences never leave your device.

What the app does not do:
- Collect personal data of any kind
- Use analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services
- Require or support user accounts
- Share data with third parties
- Use cookies or advertising identifiers

The app contains no third-party SDKs or frameworks. It is built entirely with Apple system APIs.
